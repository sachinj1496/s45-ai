Company: NEXUS BRIGHTLEARN SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED
Board Meeting held on: 10<sup>th</sup> DAY OF APRIL 2023 at 11:00 A.M.

CAPITAL REDUCTION - ALTERATION OF AUTHORISED SHARE CAPITAL

RESOLVED THAT

1) Equity Shares: 20,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each (total Rs. 2,00,000/-)
2) Redeemable Preference Shares: 20,000 Redeemable Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- each (total Rs. 2,00,000/-)

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT Clause V of the Memorandum of Association be substituted with the revised clause
upon approval by members in the Extra Ordinary General Meeting.

Proposed authorised capital (approval chain): from existing Rs. 6,50,000 /- to Rs. 4,00,000 /-.
