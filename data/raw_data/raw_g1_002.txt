# CERTIFIED TRUE COPY OF RESOLUTION (BOARD MEETING)
Company: NEXUS BRIGHTLEARN SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED
Meeting held on: 22<sup>nd</sup> DAY OF MARCH 2018 'THURSDAY' AT 1:00 PM

INCREASE IN AUTHORISED SHARE CAPITAL & ALTERATION IN THE CAPITAL CLAUSE OF MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION

1) RESOLVED THAT
pursuant to the provisions of Section 61 and 64 of the Companies Act, 2013 and other applicable provisions,
the consent of the Board of Directors is hereby accorded, subject to approvals of shareholders,
to increase the Authorized Share Capital of the Company from existing Rs. 1,50,000 (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Thousand)
divided into 15,000 (Fifteen thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each to Rs. 3,00,000 (Rupees Three Lakh)
divided into 30,000 (Thirty thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each.

2) RESOLVED FURTHER THAT
subject to approvals of shareholders, Clause V of the Memorandum of Association be substituted with the following clause:
V. The Authorised Share Capital of the Company is Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh)
divided into 30,000 (Thirty thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each.

For traceability (share breakdown):
1) Equity Shares: 30,000 Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each (total Rs. 3,00,000/-)
2) Redeemable Preference Shares: 0 Redeemable Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- each (total Rs. 0/-)

BY ORDER OF THE BOARD
Date: 22/03/2018
