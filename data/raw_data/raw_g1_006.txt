BOARD RESOLUTION PAS-3 (ALLOTMENT)
Company: NEXUS BRIGHTLEARN SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED
Board Meeting date: 15 May 2019

ALLOTMENT OF 960 EQUITY SHARES
RESOLVED THAT
pursuant to Section 62(1) of the Companies Act, 2013 and other applicable provisions,
and based on renunciation letters received from all equity shareholders,
the approval of the Board be and is hereby accorded to allot 960 equity shares of face value INR 10 each
at INR 7,860 per share (including share premium).

For traceability:
Equity Shares allotted: 960
Face value per share: INR 10
Total nominal value (issued capital basis): INR 9,600

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the requisite forms including the Return of Allotment in Form PAS 3 be filed with the Registrar.
